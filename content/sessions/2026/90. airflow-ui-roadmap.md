A year on from the launch of Airflow 3 and amidst the constantly changing world of working with AI, Airflow continues to adapt. 

We'll talk about what has changed since last summit. How we're integrating new features and plugins. What are the new uses cases for Airflow that we should design for in the future.

Come listen to some of our proposed ideas and bring your own big ideas as the second half will be an open discussion.
