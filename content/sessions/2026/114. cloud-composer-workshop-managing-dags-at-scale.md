During this workshop you are going to learn how to effectively set up a CI/CD pipeline that builds, tests, and even corrects (using Gemini) your DAGs BEFORE deploying them into your Composer environment.
You will also learn how to deploy a single custom web app to centrally control all your DAGs across many Cloud Composer environments/projects.
