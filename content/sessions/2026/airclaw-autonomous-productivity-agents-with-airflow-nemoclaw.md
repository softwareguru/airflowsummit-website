Most pipelines do exactly what you tell them. Nothing more. You write the script. You schedule the job. You fix it when it breaks. You're still the brain of the operation... and that's the problem.

What if your pipeline could think for itself?

#AirClaw is the idea that Apache Airflow and NVIDIA's NemoClaw belong together. Airflow is the schedule; it knows what needs to happen and when. NemoClaw is the agent; it knows how to get it done without you stepping in. Put them together, and you stop babysitting your workflows and start trusting them.

This talk is a blueprint — full architecture, environment setup, and the deployment path from local to production.

This would be a strategy talk with a live demo at the end.
I'll dive into the framework I've leveraged and would walk through the development of the agent, describe the architecture and showcase the agent working in real time at the end.
