Wensi Hu is a senior software engineer on LinkedIn’s Deployment Experience team, scaling LinkedIn Continuous Deployment (LCD) on Apache Airflow. Wensi drives partner migrations of Linkedin’s deployment backend services to LCD and focuses on distributed release orchestration - optimizing rollout safety, enforcing HA guardrails, and faster rollbacks - bringing repeatable, reliable deployments to thousands of services at scale.
