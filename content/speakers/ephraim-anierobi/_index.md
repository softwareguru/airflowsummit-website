Ephraim Anierobi is a software engineer and an active contributor to the Apache Airflow project. He started his journey with Apache Airflow in May 2020 as an Outreachy intern and quickly rose to become a Committer and Project Management Committee (PMC) member. Currently, he serves as a Senior Software Engineer at Astronomer.io and plays a key role as one of the release managers for Apache Airflow.
