As the Technical Lead for Declarative Pipelines at Databricks, Andreas Neumann specializes in building high-scale data infrastructure. His career spans decades of innovation at Google, Yahoo!, and IBM, as well as entrepreneurial success as co-founder of Cask Data (acquired by Google). Andreas is a respected figure in the Apache Software Foundation, having guided numerous projects through incubation. He holds a doctoral degree in Computer Science, centered on document parsing and querying.
