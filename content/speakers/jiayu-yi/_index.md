Helen Yi is a Senior Software Engineer at Stripe specializing in data orchestration and pipeline tooling. Over the past four years, she has developed expertise in managing Stripe’s highly customized Airflow environment, notably helping to drive the migration from a heavily forked Airflow 1 to Airflow 2. Currently on the Data Transformation team, Helen focuses on building tools and abstractions that make it easier for engineers to author and operate complex batch and streaming data pipelines.
