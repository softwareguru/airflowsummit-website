Volker is a Senior Developer Advocate at Astronomer with 15 years of experience architecting data platforms. Previously in the gaming industry, he built systems processing billions of daily events using Apache Airflow. Now, he leverages this expertise to define modern DataOps best practices and empower the global data community. As a regular speaker at international conferences, Volker blends deep technical knowledge with a passion for communication and knowledge sharing.
