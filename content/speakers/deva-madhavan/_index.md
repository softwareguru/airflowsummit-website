Deva S Madhavan is a Principle engineer with 20 years of experience automating silicon validation labs using robotics and software-defined workflows. 

His work spans hardware device handling, test-plan automation, pre-silicon to post-silicon validation, Servers and System software validation, UEFI and BMC bring-up, rack-scale and data-center automation and large scale lab infrastructure automation at Apple, Dell/EMC, Oracle, AMI and Qualcomm.
