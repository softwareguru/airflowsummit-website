Karthik Seshadri has worked in the software industry for over a decade and has been with Amazon for 9+ years. After initially working on Amazon devices like Kindle and Alexa, he joined AWS to help build and launch Athena Spark, AWS Glue Interactive Sessions, and AWS MWAA Serverless. He is passionate about big data and orchestration, and making large-scale data systems accessible to practitioners worldwide.
