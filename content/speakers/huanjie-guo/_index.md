Huanjie Guo is a Senior Software Engineer in the Data Infrastructure team at eBay, specializing in Apache Airflow, workflow orchestration, and metadata systems. Over the past 5 years, he has focused on platform reliability and large-scale infrastructure scalability. He also works on AI-driven automation, including automated code patching and workflow upgrade automation across eBay’s Airflow ecosystem, and has contributed to Airflow in the database cleanup area.
