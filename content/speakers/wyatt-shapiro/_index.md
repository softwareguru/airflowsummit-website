Wyatt Shapiro is currently the Lead Data Engineer at Zivian Health. Prior to this, he was the Director of Data Platform at Medically Home, where he built the data platform from the ground up and led the team through its acquisition by DispatchHealth. He loves sitting at the intersection of two fields and steering through complexity including data platforms processing gnarly datasets for easy consumption and technology enabling new healthcare delivery models.
