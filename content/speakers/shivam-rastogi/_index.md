SDE at Amazon Prime Video, where his team operates large-scale Airflow pipelines for capacity forecasting across multiple regions. Over a decade of experience building data pipeline and orchestration systems across financial services, research, and streaming. Active open-source contributor to the Apache Airflow project and volunteered at Airflow Summit 2025. He also helps build Prime Video's resiliency engineering layer including change control automation and operational metrics.
