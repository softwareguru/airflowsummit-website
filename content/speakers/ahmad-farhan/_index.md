Ahmad Farhan is a Data Engineer and open source contributor focused on data orchestration and distributed data systems. He is the creator of the Apache TinkerPop Provider for Apache Airflow, bringing native graph database support to Airflow workflows. Ahmad works as a data engineering consultant helping organisations design scalable data platforms and modern analytics architectures.
