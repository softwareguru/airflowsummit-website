Evgeny is Principal Engineer at OnsiteIQ, where he architects the computer vision and AI systems that process millions of 360° construction site images for real estate developers and investors. Before OnsiteIQ, he spent four years building self-driving perception systems for rail at Thales Canada. He holds a PhD in Robotics and Computer Vision and a BASc in Mechanical Engineering, both from the University of Toronto. He has contributed to the Apache Airflow open-source codebase.
