Vipin Kataria is an IEEE Senior Member with 20+ years in cloud architecture, machine learning, and data platforms. He holds a Master's in Data Science from UIUC. As Lead Architect - Data/ML at Picarro, he designs cloud data platforms and Lakehouse Architecture for IoT/Edge systems, covering data ingestion, governance, real-time processing, and ML pipelines for Fortune 500 environmental monitoring clients. He specializes in AI-driven analytics and ML orchestration.
