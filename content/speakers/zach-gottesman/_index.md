Since 2018, Zach has worked with Airflow as both a user and administrator. Since joining Datadog, Zach has helped integrate Airflow 3 into Datadog's internal data and analytics ecosystem, while simultaneously contributing to Apache Airflow.

In his free time, Zach makes music and writes audio effects plugins for various audio recording software suites.
