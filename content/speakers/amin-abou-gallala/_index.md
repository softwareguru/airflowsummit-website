Amin is a Data & AI Technical SME at IBM, partnering with Fortune 500 organizations particularly across financial services, retail, and healthcare to design modern data architectures spanning real-time integration, agentic data engineering, and AI-ready data foundations. His hands-on work centers on the IBM Data Integration and Data Intelligence portfolio and intersects regularly with the broader modern data stack, including Airflow, Kafka, and open lakehouse platforms. Known for making complex technology approachable, Amin has built workshops and demos used by clients across the Americas. With a background in computer engineering, he's focused on helping teams turn data pipelines into a durable driver of business outcomes.
