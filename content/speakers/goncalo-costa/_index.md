Gonçalo is a Site Reliability Engineer on the Data Products team at Miro. Over the past 4.5 years, he has transitioned from building pipelines as a Data Engineer to focusing on the underlying platform infrastructure. He is dedicated to making systems more resilient and easier to operate; a mission that has led him to oversee business-critical migrations and contribute fixes to the official Apache Airflow Helm Chart.
