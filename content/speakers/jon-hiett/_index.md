Jon Hiett is a Global Solution Architect at Broadcom focused on enterprise automation, with over 20 years of experience in IT and financial services. He defines and implements strategies for workload automation across mainframe, distributed, and cloud environments, specializing in AutoSys Workload Automation and AAI. As a featured speaker, Jon advises global enterprises on applying AI-driven insights to their automation ecosystems, emphasizing that “automation operationalizes AI”
