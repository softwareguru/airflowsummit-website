Yetunde Dada is Senior Director of Product Management at Astronomer, where she leads the build experience for Airflow, focused on developer tooling that simplifies Dag authoring and orchestration across data, analytics, and AI workflows. Previously at QuantumBlack, AI by McKinsey, she helped create Kedro, an open source framework for production ready data and ML pipelines.
