<h2>About Austin</h2>
      
<p>With a thriving startup ecosystem, a solid presence of leading tech companies, nearby universities, and an active developer population, Austin naturally supports open collaboration and data-driven problem-solving, aligning closely with the essence of the Airflow community.</p> 

<p>Beyond its tech credentials, Austin’s vibrant culture, live music scene, and welcoming atmosphere make it a compelling destination for attendees worldwide. Known for its blend of urban energy and outdoor charm, the city offers access to green spaces, trails, and a lively food scene, creating an ideal balance between professional engagement and downtime.</p>

<h2>Venue</h2>


<p><strong>The Hyatt Regency Austin</strong> is located on the shores of Lady Bird Lake at 208 Barton Springs Road, just across from downtown. Being a newly renovated hotel, it offers convenience, comfort, and scenic views with easy access to South Congress entertainment, Rainey Street nightlife, and 6th Street bars and dining. The hotel features modern rooms and suites with lake and downtown skyline views, along with in-house dining and bars ideal for networking.</p>

<p>Just outside the hotel, attendees can explore the Lady Bird Lake Trail, walk to the South Congress entertainment district, swim at the Barton Springs natural pool, or visit the iconic Congress Avenue Bridge, home to Austin’s famous bat migration at sunset during certain times of the year. Visitors can also discover live music venues, classic Texas barbecue, iconic boot stores, the Texas State Capitol, the University of Texas, and more, all offering a true taste of Austin’s culture and character.</p>
